The VBL, in six layers

How we defined the language — physical and digital, layer by layer.

Layer 01

Overall physical form

The silhouette and stance of the product family.

Layer 02

Physical points of interaction

Where and how the body invites touch.

Layer 03

Colour, material, finish, graphics

The CMF & graphic system across the range.

Layer 04

UX + UI elements & behaviours

On-device interaction patterns — my domain.

Layer 05

Approach to construction

How it's built, expressed honestly in the design.

Layer 06

Mechanical sound & feel

The tactile and acoustic signature of use.
